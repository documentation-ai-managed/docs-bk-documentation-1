## Account and Login Issues

Bk provides secure access to reconnect you with old friends, ex-classmates, neighbors, and colleagues. If you encounter login problems, follow these structured troubleshooting steps.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) after resolving initial login issues to protect your account.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Credentials" icon="check-circle">
    Verify your email and password. Use the `Forgot Password?` link to reset if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache" icon="trash-2">
    Clear your browser cache and cookies.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Chrome" icon="chrome">
        Press `Ctrl+Shift+Delete` (Windows) or `Cmd+Shift+Delete` (macOS) and select `Cookies and other site data`.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Firefox" icon="firefox">
        Go to `Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data > Clear Data`.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Incognito" icon="incognito">
    Open an incognito window and attempt login to rule out extension conflicts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact Support" icon="mail">
    Submit a ticket via the help center with your device details and error screenshot.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Data Privacy Concerns

Your data stays secure with Bk's encryption standards.

<ResponseField name="privacy_policy" field-type="url" required="true">
  Full policy at `/privacy`.
</ResponseField>

<Callout kind="alert">
  Never share sensitive info like passwords in messages.
</Callout>

Common privacy actions:

```javascript
// Example: Update visibility settings via API
const settings = {
  profileVisibility: 'connections_only',
  messageHistory: '30_days'
};
await fetch('/api/user/settings', {
  method: 'PATCH',
  body: JSON.stringify(settings)
});
```
