## Overview

Bk helps you reconnect with old friends, ex-classmates, neighbors, and colleagues effortlessly. This quickstart walks you through account creation, profile setup, searching for contacts, and making your first connection. Follow these steps to get started in under 10 minutes.

<Callout kind="tip">
  Use a strong password and verify your email immediately to unlock full features.
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Begin by signing up on the Bk platform.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit Bk" icon="globe">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://bk.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user-plus">
    Provide your email, create a password, and select your primary network (e.g., alumni, neighborhood).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link from `no-reply@bk.com` and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set Up Your Profile

A complete profile increases your connection success rate by 40%.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Photo" icon="image">
    Upload a clear profile picture (recommended: 400x400 pixels, smiling face).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill Basics" icon="edit-3">
    Enter your name, location, current job, and education history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Bio" icon="file-text">
    Write a short bio: "Marketing professional seeking alumni connections from XYZ University."
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Search and Connect

Find and reach out to potential contacts.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web" icon="globe">
    Use the search bar at the top: enter names, schools, or companies like "John Doe Harvard".
    
    Click profiles to view details, then select **Connect**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download from App Store or Google Play.
    
    Tap the magnifying glass icon, search "Jane Smith Neighborhood", and send a connection request with a note.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Columns cols={2}>
